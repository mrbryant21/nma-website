Creche & Nursery

Program Overview

Our creche program provides a warm, safe, and stimulating environment for children aged 6 months to 3 years. We understand that the early years are crucial for development, and our experienced caregivers are dedicated to nurturing each child's physical, emotional, social, and cognitive growth.

Health & Safety Measures

We prioritize the health and safety of our children with strict protocols and regular training for our staff.

We encourage regular handwashing among children and staff to maintain hygiene.

Secure Entry & Exit

Only Authorized persons can pick up children, entry and exit points are monitored
