Kindergarten Program

Nurturing young minds through play, discovery, and structured learning

Our Approach

At New Mission Academy, we provide a balanced kindergarten experience that prepares children for academic success while fostering social and emotional growth.

Safe Environment

Our classrooms are designed to be welcoming spaces where children feel secure to explore and learn.

Experienced Educators

Certified teachers specializing in early childhood development guide each child's progress.

Social Development

We emphasize cooperation, communication, and building positive relationships.

Curriculum Highlights

Pre-Reading & Pre-Math Skills

Foundational literacy and numeracy development through engaging, hands-on activities.

  • Phonemic awareness
  • Number recognition
  • Pattern identification
  • Early writing skills

Creative Arts Program

Encouraging self-expression and fine motor development.

  • Visual arts exploration
  • Music and movement
  • Dramatic play
  • Sensory activities

Physical Education

Developing gross motor skills and healthy habits.

  • Outdoor play
  • Coordination exercises
  • Team games
  • Balance activities

School Readiness

Preparing for the transition to elementary school.

  • Following routines
  • Group participation
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Independent work habits

Assessment & Progress

We use a variety of assessment methods to track each child's development:

  • Observational records
  • Portfolio collections
  • Developmental checklists
  • Parent-teacher conferences

Regular progress reports keep families informed about their child's growth in all developmental areas.
