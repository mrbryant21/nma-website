A Message from Our School Head

Dear Prospective Students, Parents, and Distinguished Visitors,

I see genius in every child

Genius is not just about intelligence and aptitude, Genius is also a word that embodies our inner soul, nature and character. Here at New Mission Academy, we celebrate the important moments in the lives of our students, their parents, and their teachers. After encountering unique characters in action throughout our journey we have gathered numerous principles of education and disciplines that aid us to be good stewards of our student's character and intellect.

At New Mission Academy, we believe that every child is unique and possesses unlimited potential. Our educational philosophy centers on creating a holistic learning environment where students develop not only academic excellence but also strong character, critical thinking skills, and the confidence to face tomorrow's challenges.
