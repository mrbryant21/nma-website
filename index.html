About New Mission Academy

Shaping Future Leaders Through Quality Education

For over a decade, New Mission Academy has been dedicated to providing exceptional education that nurtures young minds and builds character. We combine academic excellence with values-based learning.

Comprehensive Education From
Early Years to Secondary

Explore our comprehensive academic programs designed to nurture students at every stage of their educational journey. From early childhood development to junior high school preparation, each program is carefully crafted to build strong foundations and unlock every student's potential.

CRECHE

Nurturing care for our youngest learners (6 months - 2 years). We provide a safe, loving environment where infants develop through play-based activities and personalized attention.

NURSERY

Foundation years (2-3 years) focusing on social skills, basic communication, and creative exploration. Children learn through structured play and interactive activities.

KINDERGARTEN

Pre-school preparation (3-5 years) introducing basic literacy, numeracy, and social skills. Children develop independence and school readiness through guided learning.

LOWER PRIMARY

Foundation education (Primary 1-3) building core academic skills in literacy, numeracy, and science. Focus on developing critical thinking and study habits.

UPPER PRIMARY

Advanced primary education (Primary 4-6) preparing students for secondary school. Emphasis on analytical thinking, research skills, and academic excellence.

J.H.S

Junior High School (JHS 1-3) comprehensive secondary education. Students explore diverse subjects, develop leadership skills, and prepare for BECE examinations.
