Lower Primary

Building Bright Futures, One Child at a Time

Overview

At New Mission Academy, our Lower Primary program is designed to provide a solid foundation for young learners. We focus on developing essential skills in literacy, numeracy, and social interaction through engaging and interactive learning experiences.

Our dedicated teachers create a nurturing environment that fosters curiosity and a love for learning. We believe in the importance of play-based learning, allowing children to explore and discover at their own pace.

Lower Primary
Academic Journey

The Lower Primary Academic Journey at New Mission Academy builds strong foundations in core subjects and social skills. Our interactive curriculum encourages curiosity, teamwork, and personal growth, preparing students for future academic success.

Literacy Development

Building strong reading, writing, and comprehension skills through interactive and engaging activities.

Numeracy Skills

Developing mathematical concepts and problem-solving through hands-on exercises and practical applications.

Social Development

Fostering teamwork, communication, empathy, and positive social interactions through collaborative projects.

Scientific Discovery

Exploring the natural world through observation, experimentation, and inquiry-based learning activities.
