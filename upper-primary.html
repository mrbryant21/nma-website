Overview

The Upper Primary program at New Mission Academy is crafted to challenge and inspire learners as they build on foundational skills. Our curriculum emphasizes advanced literacy, numeracy, science, and critical thinking, preparing students for the next stage of their academic journey.

Experienced educators guide students through collaborative projects, leadership opportunities, and real-world problem solving. We encourage independence, responsibility, and a growth mindset, ensuring every child is equipped for future success.

Upper Primary
Academic Journey

The Upper Primary Academic Journey at New Mission Academy focuses on deepening subject knowledge, critical thinking, and independent learning. Students engage in advanced projects, collaborative activities, and leadership opportunities, preparing them for Junior High School and beyond.

Critical Thinking

Enhancing analytical skills and reasoning through advanced problem-solving and inquiry-based activities.

Mathematics

Mastering complex mathematical concepts and applying them to real-world scenarios and projects.

Leadership & Collaboration

Building leadership qualities and teamwork through group projects, presentations, and peer mentoring.

Scientific Exploration

Investigating scientific principles with hands-on experiments and research to spark curiosity and innovation.
